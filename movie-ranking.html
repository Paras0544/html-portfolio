My Top 3 movies of all time

Interstellar

2014's Interstellar is my favorite movie of all time, undoubtedly one of nolan's finest works. Set approximately 50 years in the future.It explores Mankinds biggest challanges to survive on it's own planet. A captivating story expanding through many years and many galaxies

La La Land

Ryan Gosling and Emma Stone's La La Land is a pinnacle of cinema, the relationships and life challanges are potrayed so beautifully between mia and seb, the ending might not be what everyone hoped for but it's still great.

Dune 1,2

Adapted by the book of the same name, Both the dune movies follow the world of arrakis in outer space, a planet rich with a mineral called spice which is essential for many things including interstellar travel is protectd by the people of arrakis against the harkkanans and also follows timothee chalamet's character of Paul Atredis who is termed as the Lisan-al-gaib(the holy leader) by the people of arraakis who will lead them to freedom and glory
